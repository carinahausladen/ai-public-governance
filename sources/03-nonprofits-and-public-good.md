## OpenAI

### A $50 million fund to build with communities / People-First AI Fund
Source: https://openai.com/index/50-million-fund-to-build-with-communities/ (redirects to https://openai.com/index/people-first-ai-fund/) *(reconstructed via search — verify)*

In **July 2025**, OpenAI announced a **$50M commitment** to support nonprofits and mission-focused organizations working at the intersection of innovation and public good.

- **Vision:** an early step toward ensuring "the Intelligence Age is shaped by listening, learning, and building **with**—not **for**—communities." Focus areas: education, economic opportunity, community organizing, and healthcare.
- **Eligibility:** U.S.-based public charities with valid 501(c)(3) status, operating within the 50 states or DC. OpenAI primarily considers organizations with an annual operating budget **> $500,000 and < $10 million**.
- **Grant distribution:**
  - First disbursement: **$40.5M across 208 organizations**, announced December 2025.
  - Second wave: **$9.5M** in board-directed grants slated for early 2026, targeting organizations already advancing scalable AI work in health, education, and community resilience.
